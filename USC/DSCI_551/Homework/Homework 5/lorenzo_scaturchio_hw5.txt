Find country codes of the countries where both English and French are official languages. 


cl.filter('Language == "English"').select('CountryCode').intersect(cl.filter('Language == "French"').select('CountryCode')).show()


Output:
+-----------+
|CountryCode|
+-----------+
|VUT        |
|SYC        |
